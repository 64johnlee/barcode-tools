ITF-14 is a GS1 standard 14-digit barcode used for outer shipping cases and pallets. Your 2nd ITF-14 typically represents the next level of packaging (e.g., a master carton or pallet unit). Enter your base EAN-13 and the indicator digit to auto-build the 14-digit code.
How ITF-14 is built:
[ Indicator Digit (1) ] + [ Company Prefix + Item Ref from EAN-13 (12 digits) ] + [ Check Digit (auto-calculated) ]
Indicator digit: 1 = inner pack  |  2–8 = outer/higher levels  |  9 = variable measure
