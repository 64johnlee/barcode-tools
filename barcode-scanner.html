Why a photo might not scan, and camera setup

For a photo to decode, the barcode should be sharp, level, and fill most of the frame, with a little white space each side. This tool tries the zbar engine, the browser's built-in detector, and ZXing, and retries the image at all four rotations in case it's sideways. If a photo still won't read, use the type the digits box.

Camera needs https. Browsers only allow the camera over https:// or localhost. The raw file:// or the chat preview frame may block it — hosting on GitHub Pages gives an instant https link that works on your phone. Photo and manual modes work everywhere.
